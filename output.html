Question: Find the sum of the first \(50\) terms of the arithmetic sequence where the first term is \(7\) and the common difference is \(4\).



Answer:To find the sum of the first \(50\) terms of the arithmetic sequence with the first term \(a_1 = 7\) and common difference \(d = 4\), we use the formula for the sum of the first \(n\) terms of an arithmetic sequence: \[ S_n = \frac{n}{2} \left( 2a_1 + (n - 1)d \right) \] Substituting the given values: \[ S_{50} = \frac{50}{2} \left( 2 \times 7 + (50 - 1) \times 4 \right) \] \[ S_{50} = 25 \left( 14 + 49 \times 4 \right) \] \[ S_{50} = 25 \left( 14 + 196 \right) \] \[ S_{50} = 25 \times 210 = 5250 \] **Final Answer:** \[ 5250 \]
